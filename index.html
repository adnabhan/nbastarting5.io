Price Calculation

Prices are generated using season averages for the 5 major statistical categories: Points, Rebounds, Assists, Blocks, and Steals.

By averaging the player's season averages across these categoreis (and rounding to the nearest $), we get our price

Example: Player averages 25 points, 5 rebounds, 3 assists, 1 blocks, and 1 steal, their price is $7.
